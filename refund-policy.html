Refund Policy

This policy explains when we may refund fees you have paid to Visa Nest for travel planning and visa-related administrative assistance, and how to ask us to review your case.

Last updated: 7 June 2026 · Applies to clients in the United Kingdom unless your written agreement with us says otherwise.

Overview

Visa Nest provides travel planning and visa-related administrative assistance (for example document checks, appointment guidance, and itinerary support). This Refund Policy describes how we handle refunds of fees you pay directly to Visa Nest for those services. It does not replace any written quote, engagement letter, or service agreement we agree with you in writing.

Scope

This policy applies to professional fees, service packages, or consultation charges invoiced or collected by Visa Nest. It does not govern money you pay straight to embassies, consulates, visa application centres, airlines, hotels, insurers, or other third parties—even if we helped you book or prepare those payments.

When a refund may apply

We will consider a refund or credit (in whole or in part) where, in our reasonable assessment, one or more of the following applies:

  • Duplicate payment: you were charged twice in error for the same Visa Nest service.
  • Service not started: you cancel in writing before we have begun substantive work on your matter and no third-party disbursements have been committed on your behalf.
  • Our failure to deliver agreed work: we did not provide a prepaid deliverable clearly described in your order or written agreement, and the shortfall is not due to your delay, incomplete information, or a change in law or embassy rules outside our control.
  • Goodwill: in exceptional circumstances we may offer a partial credit toward future services, even where a strict refund is not required—this is always discretionary.

We do not guarantee a particular visa outcome. Fees for professional assistance are generally not refundable solely because an embassy or authority refused an application, where we delivered the agreed preparation and submission support in line with your instructions.

Exclusions

The following are typically non-refundable through Visa Nest (you may still have separate rights against the relevant provider):

  • Government, embassy, consular, or immigration-health surcharge fees;
  • Payments to visa application centres, couriers, or priority appointment slots;
  • Flights, accommodation, tours, or insurance booked with third parties;
  • Any amount we have already paid to a supplier on your behalf where their terms are non-refundable;
  • Rush or out-of-hours surcharges after work has commenced.

How to request a refund

Email info@visanest.co.uk with the subject line “Refund request”, or use our contact form. Please include:

  • Your full name and the email address used when you paid;
  • Approximate payment date and amount, and how you paid (card, bank transfer, etc.);
  • A short explanation of why you believe a refund is due;
  • Any reference number, invoice, or agreement we sent you.

We may ask for reasonable additional information to verify your identity and locate your payment.

Processing & timing

We aim to acknowledge refund requests within 5 business days. If we approve a refund, we will normally process it to the original payment method where possible, within approximately 14 business days of approval. Bank processing times may add further delay outside our control.

If we decline a request, we will explain our reasons in writing. You may still have rights under applicable consumer law; nothing in this policy limits statutory rights that cannot be excluded.

Partial refunds

Where work has been partially completed, we may refund only the portion of our fee that fairly reflects work not yet performed, after deducting time spent, disbursements, and any non-recoverable supplier costs.

Disputes & chargebacks

If you have a concern, please contact us first so we can try to resolve it. Raising a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank without contacting us may delay resolution and could incur additional administration fees where permitted by law and card scheme rules.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when a new version is published. For ongoing matters, the version in place at the time you paid or signed your agreement will usually apply unless we notify you otherwise.

Contact us

Questions about refunds or billing should be sent to info@visanest.co.uk or to our postal address above. For data protection questions, see our Privacy Policy.

Important: Visa Nest is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. This Refund Policy is for general information. Embassy rules, carrier fare rules, and supplier terms can change without notice. For advice on your specific situation, consult a qualified professional or the relevant authority.
